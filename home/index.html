Verolog 2015

June 8-10, Vienna

The fourth meeting of the EURO Working Group on Vehicle Routing and Logistics Optimization will take place in University of Vienna, Faculty of Business, Economics and Statistics, Vienna 08-10 June 2015.

The VeRoLog conference is an annual meeting bringing together the large community of researchers and practitioners interested in vehicle routing optimization and its relations with logistics.

The conference is open to high quality methodological contributions and relevant real-world applications and case studies from the industry and the Services.

Previous Conferences

VeRoLog 2014 in Oslo, Norway, 22-25 June 2014
VeRoLog 2013 in Southampton, United Kingdom, 7-10 July 2013
VeRoLog 2012 in Bologna, Italy, 18-20 June 2012
