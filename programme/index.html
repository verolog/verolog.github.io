Keynote speakers:

  • Martin Savelsbergh, Georgia Tech, USA
  • Tolga Bektas, University of Southampton, UK

Tentative Schedule of the conference:

  • Sunday, June 7: Evening: Registration and cocktail reception at the conference site.
  • Monday June 8: Conference. Lunch provided. In the evening Conference Dinner in the Vienna City Hall.
  • Tuesday, June 9: Conference. Lunch provided. In the afternoon excursion to Kahlenberg and Klosterneuburg, ending at a Viennese Heurigen. Probably food and drinks at your own expense.
  • Wednesday, June 10: Conference. Lunch provided. In parallel, the ELOCOT - Conference on Electromobility in Logistics and Commercial Transport will take place (free for participants). Farewell event in the evening.

Please note that this info is for your planning (when to arrive and leave). We recommend arriving not later than Sunday and leaving not before Thursday. Changes may occur depending on the number of submissions.
